Meet Chloë Elodie

Hey! I'm Chloë Elodie, a 19-year-old photographer and cinematographer based in Friesland, Netherlands, but always up for travel adventures.

I'm currently in my second year at NHL Stenden, diving deeper into my love for storytelling through photography and videography.

I shine when it comes to creating imaginative (self) portraits. They're my absolute favorite to capture because there's an endless array of possibilities, leading to so many outcomes!

My Story

Cameras have been a part of me since forever. My dad used to film my sister and me every day, and I was always itching to help. That curiosity turned into a full-blown passion for photography and filmmaking. I soaked up everything I could learn about the technical side, from how light works to framing a perfect shot.

Studying art and photography was a natural step. It wasn't just about taking pictures; it was about capturing emotions and stories. And in the back of my mind was this big dream—to be a cinematographer. I wanted to create visual stories that resonated with people.

Currently, I'm fully immersed in internships, personal projects—basically anything that lets me soak up knowledge and expand my skills. Now, I'm not just that kid fascinated by a camera. I'm someone determined to turn that passion into a career, to tell stories that stick with people long after they've seen them.
